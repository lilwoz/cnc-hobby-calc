    Formulas & notes
    RPM = (Vc × 1000) / (π × D)
    Spindle speed from surface speed Vc (m/min) and tool diameter D (mm)
    Cutting feed = RPM × flutes × fzadj
    fzadj scales with engagement: lower for deep cuts and full-width slots, higher for light stepover (chip thinning)
    fzadj = fzbase × radial factor × axial factor
    Radial factor uses stepover / diameter; axial factor uses stepdown / diameter
    MRR = stepover × stepdown × cutting feed
    Material removal rate: ae × ap × feed

    Values are tuned for a 3018-PROVER (775 spindle, limited rigidity). In Fusion, set your post processor and machine limits to match. Start shallow and increase depth gradually while listening to the cut and checking chips.

    Reference values only. Always verify with an air cut and a shallow test pass before full-depth machining.
